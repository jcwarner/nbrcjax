I grew up in a suburb of Birmingham, Alabama. After high school, I enrolled and graduated from Samford University. While at Samford University, God taught me lessons I continue to learn from today. I met my wife, Jeanna, during our junior year at Samford. After getting married, we moved to Fort Worth, Texas in order for me to attend Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary. I earned two master’s degrees while at Southwestern.

Jeanna and I have 2 sons, Jordan, Jonathan, and our daughter Melea. All three of my children play the violin, while I just play the radio. I really enjoy war history and war movies, although, my wife has taught me to appreciate old movies featuring Cary Grant and others. My favorite team is the Alabama Crimson Tide.

Matthew 6:33 is my life verse. “But seek first the kingdom of God and all His righteousness and all these things will be added to you.” My prayer is that my ministry will focus on God and not on the things He will add.
