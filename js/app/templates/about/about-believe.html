The Baptist Faith and Message


Our Mission Statement:
"Let us keep in step with the Spirit,
knowing Jesus and making Him known."
"Since we live by the Spirit, let us keep in step with the Spirit." - Galatians 5:25


- We believe both the Old and New Testament scriptures to be the inerrant, infallible, & inspired Word of God.

- We believe there is one true God, and that He is revealed in all of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

- We believe that Jesus is God in flesh, the “God Man”, begotten of the Holy Spirit, was born of the virgin Mary, was tempted as we are, yet without sin. Being the perfect “Lamb of God”, He freely gave His life to redeem the sins of man. He gave Himself in death on the cross, bearing the sins of the world, arose from the grave conquering death and ascended into Heaven as the intercessor and the advocate before the Father for all those that will receive Him as Savior.

- We believe that man’s salvation/redemption was purchased by the blood of the Lord Jesus Christ on the cross of Calvary. Christ paid the sin debt for man. Salvation cannot be earned, it is the “gift of God” to all those that will freely accept it.

- We believe that the church exists to glorify God.
