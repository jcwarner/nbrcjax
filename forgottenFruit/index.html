Welcome to the Official Web site of Forgotten Fruit, Inc.  We are a 501c3 fruit recovery program that "sprouted" in 2012 in Jacksonville, Florida.  With the help of our generous donors, we have been able to help thousands of people in the Jacksonville area receive fresh and nutritious food.  Thanks for stopping by the site.  Take a look around and feel free to contact us if you feel led to be a part of the Forgotten Fruit team!
“For many months now City Rescue Mission (CRM) has received the blessing of wonderful fresh fruit from Forgotten Fruit, Inc.  CRM exists to transform lives of the homeless and needy.  We couldn’t do that without the generous hearts of “Forgotten Fruit”.  They have been an important part of the transformation of lives here at CRM.”  

         -  Penny Kievet Director, Resource Development City Rescue Mission
